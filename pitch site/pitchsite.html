The Broken System

Finance apps react after damage is done

Most apps show insights only after money is spent. They depend on user discipline instead of providing guidance, and ignore the most important moment — salary credit day.

Result: Salaries disappear silently. Savings are accidental. Control is typically lost in the first few days of the month.

Reactive apps vs FinTra

  • Others: Track spending after it happens
  • FinTra: Guides decisions before spending happens
  • Others: Show charts at month end
  • FinTra: Controls the month from salary day

The Insight

Money behavior is cyclical, not daily

If guidance does not begin when salary is credited, everything else becomes damage control.

  • Day-1 control matters more than month-end charts
  • Prevention beats correction
  • A cycle must be closed, not abandoned

FinTra is built as a money operating system for salaried users — not a diary of expenses.
