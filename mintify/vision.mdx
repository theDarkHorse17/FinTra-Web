
FinTra aims to become India’s first behaviour-correcting salary companion.

### Long-Term Vision
- India’s “personal CA” for every salaried individual
- An app that reduces emotional overspending
- Real-time heads-up salary management
- AI-driven recommendations based on user patterns
- Financial stability for India’s Gen-Z workforce

### North Star Metrics
- Daily Active Salary Users (DASU)
- 30-day retention
- Monthly savings %
- Category consistency score
