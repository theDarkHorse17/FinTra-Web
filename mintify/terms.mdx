# **FinTra — Master Terms**
### *(Founder-Protective Framework)*

These Master Terms define the operating principles, ownership rules, contribution boundaries, and engagement expectations for anyone working with, contributing to, or interacting with **FinTra**.

---

## **1. Founder Authority Terms**
- Founder retains full and final authority over all decisions.
- No contributor, contractor, advisor, or investor may override Founder decisions.
- All strategic direction flows from the Founder.

---

## **2. Ownership & Equity Terms**
- 100% equity remains with the Founder unless explicitly granted in a signed agreement.
- No equity is granted for ideas, advice, support, or informal collaboration.
- Any future equity follows a written vesting schedule.
- No entitlement to equity without a formal contract.

---

## **3. Intellectual Property (IP) Terms**
- All code, design, strategy, architecture, branding, or documentation created for FinTra becomes exclusive property of FinTra.
- Contributors cannot reuse or repurpose FinTra assets.
- All work must be delivered to Founder-controlled repositories.

---

## **4. Confidentiality Terms**
- All internal information is confidential and cannot be shared externally.
- Confidentiality obligations survive even after engagement ends.

---

## **5. Contribution Terms**
- All work must align directly with Founder instructions.
- No unilateral feature additions or changes.
- All tools, libraries, and tech choices require Founder approval.

---

## **6. No Claim / No Interference Terms**
- No contributor may claim the title of cofounder unless granted formally.
- No contributor may speak on behalf of FinTra publicly.
- No interference in hiring, product direction, brand identity, or user communication.

---

## **7. Compensation Terms**
- Contractors are paid for completed deliverables only.
- No advance payments unless explicitly stated.
- Compensation does not grant equity or ownership.

---

## **8. Termination Terms**
- Founder may terminate any engagement immediately.
- Grounds include missed deadlines, poor quality, misalignment, breach of confidentiality, or misconduct.
- Upon termination, all access is revoked and all work done remains FinTra property.

---

## **9. Team Expansion Terms**
- Hiring will occur in future as scale requires.
- Early team members join under a probation structure.
- Leadership roles are performance-earned.
- Founder maintains overarching strategic control.

---

## **10. Investor Terms (High-Level)**
- Founder retains majority control and strategic authority.
- Early capital enters via SAFE/CCPS to avoid immediate dilution.
- No investor gains operational or product-level authority.

---

## **11. Product & Vision Terms**
- FinTra's philosophy and behavioural-finance foundation are protected.
- No external party may alter core product values.
- Brand and roadmap direction remain under founder stewardship.

---

## **12. Dispute Resolution Terms**
- Disputes are resolved privately and internally first.
- Final decision rests with the Founder.
- Legal jurisdiction: India.

---

These terms form the backbone of FinTra’s structure — ensuring clarity, protection, and founder-driven direction as the company grows.
